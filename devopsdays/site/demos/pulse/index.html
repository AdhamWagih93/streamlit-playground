The takeaway

The most advanced automation in 2026 isn't pointed at infrastructure — it's pointed at people.

Sustainable pace is an engineering metric now — measured, alerted on, and remediated like latency or uptime. Burnout stops being a personal weakness someone has to confess to, and becomes what it always was: a system failure we can detect and fix. The on-call rotation rebalances itself, toil flows to agents, and a tired human gets a quiet morning back — automatically, before the crash. A team you don't grind into the ground ships more, for longer.
