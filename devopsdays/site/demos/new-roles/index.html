The takeaway

The DevOps role didn't get automated away — it got promoted.

The work moved from DOING the toil to DESIGNING the systems (and agents) that do the toil — plus owning the judgement calls machines can't make: ethics, tradeoffs, and human sustainability. The half-life of any specific tool skill is shrinking fast. The skills that stay durable are systems thinking, judgement, and learning velocity. You don't have to know every tool. You have to keep moving up a level.
